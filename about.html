About Me

My name is Ciaran Mallory and I am a 20 year old aspiring Electronic Engineer and Software developer. I am an extremely hardworking, dedicated and driven individual, who exceeds at problem solving. I enjoy learing new technologies and I learn by doing so I have built have a wide variety of projects which can be found on my Github. I am always curious about how things work. I work well in a team and have excellent leadership skills due to my background in Kayak Instructing.

My engineering journey began when I was in primary school. I have always been fascinated by engineering and I knew that I wanted to help influence the future. Once I graduated secondary school, I completed a Level 8 Bachelors of Engineering in Electronic Engineering at Limerick Institute of Technology. I developed an interest in software development and throughout the course of my time at LIT I taught myself as much as I could about software.
